Community Support

General Community Fund

Support the safety net, tree planting, Bean Camp, La Louisiane, and everything else we do to make New Orleans a little better every day — and a lot more resilient when things get hard.

Where your donation goes

  • 🎒Bean Camp — Zero-barrier summer camp for New Orleans kids, with teens earning real income as counselors
  • 🎵La Louisiane — Free Cajun music and French language nights, paying musicians and preserving culture
  • 🌿More Trees in the Bywater — Planting shade, reducing heat, and building a greener neighborhood
  • ❤️Community Safety Net — Emergency support for families, elders, and musicians when disaster strikes

Climate & Neighborhood

More Trees in the Bywater

A partnership between the Krewe of Red Beans and the Bywater Neighborhood Association. The Bywater has one of the lowest tree canopy rates in New Orleans — and no one will fix this but us.

Why trees matter here

The Bywater has one of the lowest tree canopy rates in the city. This makes heat waves significantly worse for our neighborhood — the urban heat effect is stronger without shade. Trees also help reduce flooding.

If we each give a few dollars a month, we can transform our neighborhood. Every tree planted is shade for a neighbor, cleaner air, and a little more resilience against the next heat wave or hurricane.

Do you have a great spot for a sidewalk tree? We'd love to help. Email us at beans@kreweofredbeans.org

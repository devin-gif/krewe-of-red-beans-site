Lundi Gras

Join the Parade

The Krewe of Red Beans parades every Lundi Gras — the Monday before Mardi Gras. It's the heart of everything we do. If you're a member, you can walk with us.

Parade Dues

Parade dues help cover the cost of the parade, supplies, and Lundi Gras celebrations. Dues are separate from general membership — pay what you like.

Member Parade Dues $30 min  ·  $125 suggested per adult
Pay what you like Any amount from $30 and up — every dollar helps the parade roll
Due by Two weeks before Lundi Gras
